About the Building

C Street Plaza is a modern, light‑filled professional building designed to support growing businesses with flexible, turnkey office solutions. Large windows, updated finishes, and thoughtfully planned common areas create a welcoming environment for both staff and clients. Tenants enjoy access to two shared conference rooms, affordable printing and copying services, free scanning, a fully equipped break room, and on‑site janitorial support—all included to simplify your operations and reduce overhead.
